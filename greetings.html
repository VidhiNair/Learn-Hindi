Greetings

Namaste - Hello

Aap ka naam kya hai? - What is your name?

Mera naam _ hai - My name is _

Aap kaise ho? - How are you?

Meh theek hoon. - I am fine.

Aap kitne sal ki ho? - How old are you?

Meh _ sal ki hoon. - I am _ years old.

Aap ko kya acha lagta hai? - What do you like?

Mujhe _ acha lagta hai. - I like _.

Aap kaha rehate ho ? - Where do you live?

Aap ka school kohn sa hai? - Which is your school?

Mujhe pasand nahi - I do not like.

Mujhe pasand hai. - I like.

Mujhe paata nahi - I don't know.

Aapne khayal rakhna - Take care.

Phir Millengeh - See you later.
